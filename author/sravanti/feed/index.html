The ‚ÄúDiscover‚Äù feature was released about a year ago, expanding the app beyond sharing photos and videos between personal networks of friends. ‚ÄúDiscover‚Äù stories allow users to explore daily stories by publication outlets such as Vice, Mashable, National Geographic, Vox and most recently, WSJ. Snapchat Discover stories are structured as a slideshow which the user can swipe through. Lately, publishers have been taking to Snapchat to engage with users in a more typical Snapchat fashion by posting daily stories. Users can add publishers by username (for example, ‚Äúnpr‚Äù). This particular use of Snapchat lends itself to a more interactive experience ‚Äî for example, NPR will often explicitly solicit feedback from users viewing a Snapchat story, and users can send photos, videos or text in response. As for the implications Snapchat has for the future of news and storytelling, it‚Äôs clear that Snapchat is a medium which encourages interactivity in a different medium than other forms of social media ‚Äî not only among friends, but now between users and publications, which previously was a large barrier.
